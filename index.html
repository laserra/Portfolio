LEVIBOM

In this project the client wanted a integration of different services, including web based and print services. The website is made in PHP with a MySQL feed for client and product database. This includes a virtual store for home delivery.
All the interaction and slideshows are made with Flash and AS3. In the case of the slideshows they are fed with an XML file. Also for this work I made a TV information interface to display on two Full HD LCD tv's. These show pictures of products, but can include animations in SWF format, weather information and latest news. Both are fed with RSS.
It includes also a module with cultural events, but this can be replaced with other type of information module

  • Client: LEVIBOM - Fine bakery and food products
  • Date: February 2009
  • Service: Web Development and Print

Almanaque da Républica - 100th anniversary of the republic

Portugal celebrated it's 100th anniversary of the republic.
The national news agency Lusa, wanted a website to show articles made by their journalists and collaborators about the event.
Many stories about important figures, events and many more could be read at this site. It has a timeline made in Flash and AS3, completely dinamic, and adapted to touchscreens with a smooth movement for easy navigation. It has another event line with important stories, an agenda with official events for this commemoration and a multimedia gallery capable of showing image, video and SWF animations. All the feed is made by PHP and a MySQL database.

  • Client: Lusa - News Agency Portugal
  • Date: May 2010
  • Service: Web Development

OrderIt

My first mobile game project. This is a simple puzzle with just a few rules. Time and concentration are key to go far.
Try to tap the rectangles in order under a certain amount of time. You have a few seconds to achieve this, and the order can either be ascending or descending... did I mention that the numbers disappear just in time for you to memorize where they are? :)
